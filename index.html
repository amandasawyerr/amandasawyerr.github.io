~RODENTS I LIKE~

BEAVER | SQUIRREL | PORCUPINE | GROUNDHOG

Why I Love Rodents :)

Types of Rodents I Like

Beavers

Beavers have webbed feet and leathery tails

North American Beaver

A baby beaver named Pinecone, slurping down his/her breakfast

Did you know? Beavers need to be x-rayed to determine their gender

Beavers are very family oriented, and stay with their parents for 2 years, helping to raise their parents newest litter when they are 1yrs old

Squirrels

Squirrels are fast and use there tails as blankets when they sleep

Squirrels in Canada

A sweet grey squirrel

Did you know? There are 6 different types of tree squirrels in Canada

They use their tails for millions of things - communicating, balancing, and as a warm blanket!

Porcupines

Porcupines make nice noises and can climb up high

North American Porcupine

A hunrgy porcupine named Ricky

Did you know? Porcupines are extremely vocal, it is not uncommon for them to scream upon being touched! Babies are also very noisy when drinking their milk

Also! There quills are soft when they are born, and harden within ~24hrs of being born

Groundhogs

Groundhogs are sweet and snuggly and dig as well as climb

Groundhog

A tiny groundhog

Did you know? Groundhogs are amazing climbers! Don't be suprised if you see one scaling up a tree

They are considered to be a ground squirrel species
